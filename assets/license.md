## 2. About eCOOP SUITE

**eCOOP SUITE** is a next-generation, integrated cloud-based cooperative management platform. It transforms the cooperative experience for members, clients, staff, and officers by providing a friendly, intuitive, and accessible digital platform.

It includes modules that may cover, but are not limited to:

* Core Cooperative Management
* Member Information System
* Savings and Deposits Management
* Loans and Credit Processing
* Accounting and Financial Reporting
* Teller and Cashiering Operations
* POS and Transaction Processing
* Administrative and Audit Controls
